Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book a photography session?

+

For portrait sessions, I recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred date and time. Fall sessions and spring mini-sessions tend to book up quickly, often 2-3 months in advance. For newborn sessions, the best time to book is during your second trimester to guarantee availability around your due date.

What happens if it rains on the day of our outdoor session?

+

Don't worry! I always keep a close eye on the weather forecast and will contact you 24-48 hours before your session if inclement weather is expected. We can either reschedule for a different day or, depending on your preference, adapt to an indoor location. I'm very flexible when it comes to weather-related rescheduling.

Do you travel for photography sessions?

+

Absolutely! I serve the greater New England area and am happy to travel to your preferred location. Sessions within 30 miles of Fairfield, Maine are included in the standard session fee. For locations beyond that radius, a small travel fee applies based on distance. For destination sessions outside of New England, please contact me for a custom quote.

How and when do I pay for my photography session?

+

A 50% non-refundable retainer is required at the time of booking to secure your session date and time. The remaining balance is due one week prior to your session. I accept payment via credit card, Venmo, or bank transfer. After your initial inquiry, I'll send a digital booking proposal that includes all payment details and makes it easy to complete your booking online.

How long until I receive my photos?

+

Your online gallery will be delivered within 2-3 weeks of your session date. During peak seasons (fall and spring), delivery may take up to 4 weeks. For weddings and events, the timeline is 4-6 weeks. If you need images by a specific deadline, please let me know in advance, and I'll do my best to accommodate rush requests.

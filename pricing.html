Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pricing and sessions

Is a deposit required to book a session?

+

Yes, a non-refundable retainer of 50% of the session fee is required to reserve your date. The remaining balance is due one week prior to your session. The retainer ensures I hold your date exclusively for you and begin the preparation process.

Do you offer mini sessions?

+

Yes! I offer seasonal mini sessions several times throughout the year (typically spring, fall, and holiday). These are 20-minute sessions at a set location with 10-15 digital images included. Mini sessions are announced to my email list first and typically book quickly. Sign up for my newsletter to be notified when these become available.

Can I purchase additional digital images?

+

Yes! Additional digital images beyond what's included in your collection can be purchased for $35 each or $250 for all remaining images from your gallery. Many clients choose to upgrade to all digital images after seeing their complete gallery.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Absolutely. For print orders over $500, I offer 3-month payment plans at no additional cost. For large wall art collections or complete wedding packages, we can arrange 6-month payment plans. All payment plans require a credit card on file.

Do you offer military or first responder discounts?

+

Yes, I offer a 10% discount on session fees for active military, veterans, first responders, and teachers. Please mention your service when booking and provide appropriate ID at or before your session. This discount applies to session fees only and cannot be combined with mini sessions or special promotions.

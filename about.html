About Me

The person behind the lens

Hello, I'm Emma

A photographer with a passion for capturing authentic moments and natural beauty.

Based in the scenic countryside of Fairfield, Maine, I specialize in portrait and nature photography that embodies the modern farmhouse aesthetic â€” clean, timeless, and warmly inviting.

My journey with photography began over a decade ago when I received my first DSLR camera as a gift. What started as a hobby quickly blossomed into a lifelong passion and eventually, a fulfilling career. I found myself drawn to the way photography allows us to preserve fleeting moments and emotions, creating tangible memories that can be cherished for generations.

I believe that the best photographs happen when people feel comfortable and truly themselves. That's why I prioritize creating a relaxed, enjoyable experience for every client. Whether we're capturing your family's milestone moments or the subtle beauty of a landscape at golden hour, my goal is to create images that tell your unique story with authenticity and artistic vision.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring hiking trails with my golden retriever Scout, tending to my vegetable garden, or renovating my 1890s farmhouse. These experiences in nature and with historic spaces deeply influence my photographic style and creative approach.

My Approach

What makes my photography experience unique

Authentic Connections

I believe great photographs come from genuine moments. Every session begins with getting to know you and creating a comfortable, relaxed environment where your personality can shine through.

Natural Settings

I gravitate toward outdoor locations with beautiful natural light and organic textures. From golden fields to rustic barns, these settings provide the perfect backdrop for the modern farmhouse aesthetic that defines my work.

Timeless Editing

My editing style emphasizes clean, true-to-life colors with a warm, inviting tone. I avoid trendy filters or heavy processing that might look dated in the years to come, ensuring your images remain timeless.

Joyful Experience

Photography sessions should be fun! I keep things lighthearted and enjoyable, with gentle guidance rather than stiff posing. The result is natural smiles and genuine interactions that make for beautiful, heartfelt images.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about accessing and using your gallery

How long will my gallery be available?

+

Your online gallery will remain active for 12 months from the date it's delivered to you. After that time, galleries are archived and may require a small fee to reactivate. I recommend downloading your images soon after receiving your gallery to ensure you have permanent access to them.

Can I share my gallery password with family and friends?

+

Absolutely! Your gallery password can be shared with family members and friends so they can view and even order prints directly from your gallery. The password protection is simply to keep your images private from the general public.

What if I need help ordering prints or products?

+

I'm happy to assist you with print ordering or product selections. Simply email me or use the contact form, and we can schedule a video call to go through your gallery together. I can provide recommendations for print sizes, frame styles, or album designs based on your specific needs and the images in your collection.

Can I download all my photos at once?

+

Yes! There's a "Download All" button in your gallery that allows you to download all your images as a zip file. Depending on the number of images and your internet connection speed, this may take several minutes. For the best experience, use a computer rather than a mobile device for downloading multiple images.

Why do my photos look different when printed elsewhere?

+

Color calibration and paper quality vary significantly between printing services. The professional lab I partner with uses calibrated printers and premium archival papers that ensure your images look exactly as they were edited. Consumer print services often have automated color adjustments that can alter the look of your images. For the highest quality and color accuracy, I recommend ordering prints through your gallery.

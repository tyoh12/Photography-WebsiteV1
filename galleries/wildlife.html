Wildlife Photography

Capturing Maine's magnificent wild creatures

Discover the untamed spirit of Maine's wildlife through my photography collection. Each image represents hours of patience, respect for nature, and a deep appreciation for these remarkable creatures.

My approach to wildlife photography combines technical precision with artistic composition, seeking to showcase these animals in their natural environment with minimal disturbance. I'm particularly drawn to capturing fleeting moments that reveal personality, behavior, and the interconnectedness of wildlife with their habitat.

Many of these images are available as limited edition fine art prints. Visit the pricing page for details on print options and limited editions.

My Wildlife Photography Ethics

The welfare of my wild subjects always comes first. I follow a strict ethical code when photographing wildlife:

Respect & Distance

I maintain a respectful distance and use telephoto lenses to avoid disturbing natural behaviors. I never chase, bait, or manipulate wildlife for a photograph.

Habitat Conservation

I stay on established trails, leave no trace, and never damage habitat for a better angle. A portion of my wildlife print sales supports local conservation efforts.

Education & Advocacy

Through my images, I hope to foster appreciation and respect for our wild neighbors, promoting conservation and responsible wildlife viewing practices.
